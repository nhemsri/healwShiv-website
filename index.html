Heal with Shiv

HealwithShiv is a platform dedicated to awakening and nurturing the inner child within each individual, guiding them toward bliss and personal excellence. By blending alternative healing modalities with evidence-based western therapeutic approaches, we offer a holistic and eclectic path to healing the mind, body, and soul.

About Me

Shiv is a certified therapist with over 10 years of experience transforming lives and promoting mental wellness and spiritual well-being. A former law enforcement professional in prisons and correctional facilities, Shiv now dedicates his time as a full-time therapist, coach, and healer. He specializes in teaching and training individuals and organizations on behavioral and functional aspects that drive excellence, psychological resilience, and spiritual growth.

An avid traveler, Shiv is deeply passionate about people, cultures, geopolitics, science, astronomy, and books—always seeking to explore and learn from the world around him.
